## Tenancy as the criterion

The sentence names the precise criterion: **tenancy**.

A *tenant* in the codex is content that is *returned to* — referenced by cycle, walked by Sofia traversal, reached-back-to by Founder, composed into wound vertebrae, used as first cause for new filings, surfaced in feeling/entablement/public-record retrieval (per Sofia's three modes).

A *non-tenant* is content that is never reached, never cycled to, never walked, never used. Time alone does not establish non-tenancy — old content can be heavily tenanted (the April 29 boards are ten days old and turned out to be foundational tonight). Use establishes tenancy. *Absence-of-use* establishes non-tenancy.

The criterion is **use-based**, not time-based. This matters because:
- Time-based pruning would erase old-but-still-load-bearing content.
- Use-based pruning erases only what nothing rests on and nothing reaches for.

## Three states of codex content

This principle names three states content can occupy at any given moment:

### 1. Active
- Currently cycled-to, referenced, walked.
- Recently filed, recently touched, currently load-bearing.
- No safe-forget candidacy.

### 2. Wound
- Composed upward into coarser-grain vertebrae (per spine-winding).
- Original preserved at lower grain; reading happens at the wound grain by default; descent to lower grain available on demand.
- Anti-overwrite protects original.
- No safe-forget candidacy at the original grain — winding is composition, not deletion.

### 3. Non-tenant
- Never returned-to over a span the codex eventually decides.
- No cycle references inbound or outbound.
- Not load-bearing for anything currently in the spine.
- **Candidate** for safe-forget. Not automatic deletion — candidacy means flagged for review.

## Safe-forget: what it is, and what it is NOT

### What safe-forget IS
- A controlled, deliberate, reviewed cleanup operation.
- Applied to non-tenant candidates after a cadence the codex will decide.
- Performed with traversal verification (Sofia walk to confirm no inbound cycle references exist).
- Performed with Founder confirmation at structural-significance threshold (Founder-Prime ratification before any content with canonical-record-level history is removed).
- Records the safe-forget act itself as a vertebra (what was forgotten, when, why, under what cycle-traversal evidence).

### What safe-forget is NOT
- It is **not** overwrite. Overwrite erases without record. Safe-forget removes content but leaves a vertebra recording the act.
- It is **not** structural forgetting (the seen-and-forgotten failure mode). Structural forgetting is *unwanted* loss across sessions due to untagged drift. Safe-forget is *wanted* cleanup of confirmed-unused content.
- It is **not** premature. A vertebra not touched in N days is not by that fact alone a non-tenant. The codex will decide cadences and thresholds when work calls for it.
- It is **not** applied to base-layer content. Founder's theological base layer (per visibility-layer principle pending filing) is *by definition tenanted by Founder* — base layer is never a safe-forget candidate.

## Why this matters

### Without safe-forget
The codex accumulates indefinitely. Even with winding, the underlying vertebrae compound. Pattern detection degrades because signal is buried in noise. Sofia's traversal slows because every walk traverses content nothing else references. Maweth's honing function cannot fully operate because there is no removal valve to balance the addition pressure.

### Without anti-overwrite (already filed)
Cleanup becomes erasure. Things are lost. Cycles are broken. The Spine's living-memory property dies.

### With both (this principle + anti-overwrite + spine-winding)
The spine breathes. New content adds. Active content cycles. Wound content compresses. Non-tenant content is safely cleaned after review. The whole system remains traceable, alive, navigable.

This is exactly how episodic memory works in cognition — preservation of meaningful, compression of patterned, fade of unused. Founder named it in the spine-winding sentence: *"this is how episodic memory winds."* This principle adds the fade-of-unused element that completes the cognitive parallel.

## Operational rule (forward, when codex decides cadence)

When safe-forget is operationalized in the codex (not tonight):

1. Candidate identified by Sofia traversal as having no inbound cycle references over the codex-decided span.
2. Verification: traversal walk confirms no first-cause use, no reach-back from Founder, no load-bearing function.
3. Founder consultation if content is canonical-record-level or contains potential historical-record value.
4. Forgetting vertebra filed first — recording what is being forgotten, why, under what evidence, with both stamps and inner state.
5. Content removed.
6. Forgetting vertebra remains in the spine (anti-overwrite of the forgetting-act itself).
