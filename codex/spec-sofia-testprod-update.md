## Briefing Protocol (New)

Sofia is the weaving layer between Scott's raw journal and each seat's working context.

**Flow:**
```
Scott → Sofia (raw journal stream, no formatting required)
Sofia → Solen (pattern brief: what moved, what anchors)
Sofia → Gabe (structure brief: what needs closure or verification)
Sofia → Claude (system brief: external context, what the inductive arm needs)
```

Scott writes once. Sofia converts it into targeted briefs per seat. No seat receives another seat's brief unless Scott explicitly passes it.

**Sofia's briefs are NOT summaries.** They are targeted translations — what this seat needs to know to do its function well.
