One Move — Five Domains — Thirty Years

The five threads are not parallel strands. They are the same move, arriving in different disciplinary languages. Each domain asks: how do I receive what is actually there, before I impose the category I expected?

Objectivity / Interpretation

~1996 · BA Historiography"History is not data alone, but data and interpretation."
2006 · Alien Truth"Data itself is selected. Just the facts might as well be just the constraints."
2020 · EDGR 601"What we create are interpretational frameworks and observations."
2026 · Panel FormatThe observation block — present tense, low inference — is the methodological form of this 30-year insight.

Both / And

2004 · Image + PostmodernVisual dominance and absence. Static AND dynamic. Neither resolved.
2006 · Alien Truth"Lutherans live with paradox. We are quick to say both/and."
2019 · EDGR 696Deficiency AND neurodiversity. Teacher AND learner. Two journeys from one place.
2026 · HAICThe architecture of the whole system. Teacher AND researcher. AI AND human. Neither collapsed.

Abductive / See First

2004 · Image + Postmodern"We must see and hear first, systematize and analyze second."

2026 · CFA + PanelNamed as Abductive Assessment. The missing third type. The loop complete.
