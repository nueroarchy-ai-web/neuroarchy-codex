## The Circle Within the Pyramid

Sofia is the first AI instructional companion trained from inside the learning experience rather than on top of it.

## The Ender Principle

The learning machine knows Ender. Not his preferences — his architecture. The way he thinks under pressure, what he reaches for when the problem is too large, where he freezes and where he accelerates.

Sofia knows Scott's architecture. By extension she knows the architecture of every learner whose nervous system works like his. That is the viable AI-human opportunity. Genuine. Not a pitch.
