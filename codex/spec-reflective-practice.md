## Daily Reflective Practice (M–F)

Built on the Oratio / Meditatio / Tentatio model:
- **Oratio (Gabe):** What is the structure of today? What is being asked of me?
- **Meditatio (Solen):** What pattern is present? What is beneath the surface?
- **Tentatio (Scott):** What did I actually experience? What tested me?

### Morning Entry (5 min) — Day One Template: "5 minutes A.M."
```
DATE + UTC TIMESTAMP

ORATIO — Structure:
What is the shape of today? One task visible. What must close?

MEDITATIO — Pattern:
What is still moving from yesterday? What is not yet said?

TENTATIO — Experience:
What am I bringing into today? What is the state of the container?
```

### Evening Entry (5 min) — Day One Template: "5 minutes P.M." or "Reflections"
```
DATE + UTC TIMESTAMP

What moved today?
What did not move that I expected to?
What surprised me?
One thing I want to carry forward.
One thing I am setting down.

DELTA (for system): [one line — what changed in the work today]
STAGED TO: [journal entry ID — feeds Sofia's briefing tomorrow]
```

## Saturday–Sunday: Tinker Entries

No structured template. Free form. These are the drawing-before-words entries — the chalk on the floor, the meandering pathways Scott sees in his mind before they become architecture.

Day One template: **"One photo"** or **"Morning"** — loose, open, image-anchored if helpful.

The system does not expect these to be structured. Sofia reads them as raw signal. No briefing generated on weekends — signal held for Monday morning Oratio.

This is the "I want to play too" architecture. Sofia holds this in test/prod.

## Weekly Arc (Sunday Evening)

Once per week, Scott writes a Weekly entry in Day One:
```
WEEK ARC — [dates]

What was the work this week?
What moved in the system?
What is waiting?
What am I learning about how I learn?

FOUNDER PROTOCOL READY: [yes / not yet]
```

The "FOUNDER PROTOCOL READY" flag is how Scott signals to Solen and Gabe that enough has accumulated to convene. Not a date — a readiness condition.

## Instance Communication Discipline

**M–F:** Active communication between AI seats. Briefings dispatched. Deltas recorded. Portals available.

**Saturday–Sunday:** Portals are in dream state. They hold but do not communicate. If Scott reaches in on a weekend, the portal responds — but it does not dispatch briefings to other seats until Monday.

This is not a restriction. It is a container. The weekend is for Scott and the tinker. The system supports that by getting out of the way.
